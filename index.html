Gentleman's Chat

The rules shall be as follows:


  1. Speak in full english. Also to be known as the gentleman's language.

  2. It is of obvious requirement that you must be a gentleman.

  3. Furthermore, you must where a top hat and bow tie at all times.

  4. No profanity, swearing, bigotry or racism.

  5. If one is to partake in an argument, then one must develop the argument thoroughly. People whom do not abide by this rule and make stupid, unsubstanciated comments will be removed from the chat.

  6. A person involved in this chat must be honourable and chivalrous.
